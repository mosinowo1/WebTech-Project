Project Proposal

What is the project about?

My project is going to be a fashion blog focused on personal style. I will be sharing my techiques and tools I use to style my personal closet, shopping hacks, and more.

What is the objective of your website?

The objective of my website is to share my passion for fashion with others in the hopes of creating a community.

Who is your target audience?

My target audience is anyone between the ages of 18 and 35 who is interested in learning more about fashion, or is already into fashion.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

I plant to use photo content, video content, and more, to show the inspiration behind my personal fashion style.
